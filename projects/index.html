Axure: Dance App

In this project, I, along with my fellow groupmates, used Axure to build an app the we have been designing and researching for. We interviewed many dancers and some non-dancing people in order to gain insight and later feedback to better improve on our design. The app allows a user to interact with dance communities in their area or of their interest. It allows them to easily communicate with other members and take their dance videos and allow them to smoothly send it to the captain of the team in order to receive feedback on their progress, which can also be seen on the app. The captain can comment on a specific portion of the video and resend it to the user for them to view.
